## 👨‍💻 About Me

I am a First-Year B.Tech student specializing in Electronics and Telecommunication Engineering at K.K. Wagh Institute of Engineering Education and Research. I am passionate about bridging the gap between hardware and software, focusing on practical IoT solutions, embedded systems, and full-stack web development. 

Instead of just learning theory, I actively build. From designing underwater sonar mapping prototypes and 3lb combat robots to managing my own VPS deployments and setting up web apps, I love tearing things apart and figuring out how to make them work better.

*   **Embedded Systems & IoT:** Prototyping with ESP32, Arduino, and integrating sensors (LiDAR, Sonar, IMU) for real-world data collection.
*   **Web & Software Dev:** Building out platforms and APIs using React, JavaScript, C/C++, and FastAPI.
*   **Infrastructure:** Managing Minecraft servers, using Pterodactyl/Wings, and deploying projects via Fly.io and n8n.
*   **Open To:** Collaborations on hardware/software projects, hackathons, and learning more about Edge AI architectures.
